Solo Jazz Academy

Take Your Dancing to the Next Level!

Solo Jazz Academy shows you how to take your dancing from average to badass.

Our progressive 10 week series courses you will provide the instruction you need to massively improve your dancing. Idalia Ramos is a nationally recognized solo jazz dancer who is excited to share her awesome moves and secrets with you! You will also improve your partner dancing by learning moves and styles that you can add within a partner dance to make it extra fun and musical. Can't commit to a 10 week series? Idalia is also offering private lessons for groups up to 4 people so you can schedul your own weekly lesson!

Beginner Track

Starts Tuesday February 16, 2016

Every Tuesday 6:00pm-7:30pm

This track will introduce you to all of the classic solo jazz moves you need to know! You will learn the world famous line dance called the Shim Sham so you can cut a rug with everyone at the next swing dance you attend. Come ready to sweat and have a great time!

Intermediate/Advanced Track

Starts Tuesday February 16, 2016

8:00pm-9:30pm

This track will focus on quality of movement, musicality and style. Learn how to take the classic moves and line dances and make them your own! We will discuss theory, explore ranges, rhythms and how to connect with the music when both solo and partnered dancing. Come ready to sweat and have a great time!

Private or Semi-Private Lessons

Schedule a customized lesson on your own time! Can be a private or small group lesson with up to 4 people with similar dancing abilities. Work on musicality, style, performance, or whatever you'd like to work on!

About Your Instructor

Idalia has been teaching Lindy Hop and Solo Jazz in the Bay Area since 2012. Since then she has won numerous contest, including 1st at the Open Jack and JIll at ILHC, at the Open Solo Jazz Contest at Stompology and recently 1st as well at the Fog City Stomp Solo Jazz Battle! She loves to help people have a great time and enjoy themselves on the dance floor.
