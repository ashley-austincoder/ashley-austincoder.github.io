30 Day No Alcohol Challenge

Posted by Ashley Smith

February 16, 2016

Today I completed a 30 day no alcohol challenge! It was a challenge for me, but once I committed myself I found that it got easier and not-drinking has a lot of amazing benefits. I ended up making several new friends out at bars since I was able to remember people and conversations clearly. I started dating someone I like, the first date was scary at first but now we know we actually get along without alcohol! I was able to workout I also saved a LOT of money compared to my friends who did drink when we went out.
